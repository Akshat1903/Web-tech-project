Classroom activities turns online.

Classrooms

Create your own classroom and add students and enjoy all the features of classweb. All the classroom activites turned online with classweb makes classroom interaction more simple and interesting.

Digital Learning

Online education is a flexible instructional delivery system that encompasses any kind of learning that takes place via the Internet. Class Web gives educators an opportunity to reach students who may not be able to enroll in a traditional classroom course and supports students who need to work on their own schedule and at their own pace.

Community

Learning is not just about teachers and students. Class Web allows you to bring everyone together—students, teachers, coaches, parents, administrators—with one communication and collaboration platform.

How To Use

Login and Sign Up

If you are a teacher or you want to create your own classroom, then Sign up as a teacher. If you are a student and want to get added in a classroom, then Sign Up as student and enjoy our facilities.

Create your Classroom.

As a teacher create your own classroom and add your students. Upload assignments, course materails, class notice in your classroom and award marks to your students based on their performance.

Upload Assignments

Upload your assignments and give due date to students for their submissions. Collect their submissions online and award them marks according to their work.

Course Materials

Upload your course materials to make learning easy for your students. Your uploaded course materials will be accessible to students in your classroom. Students can download the materials and study easily.

Marks Evaluation

Award marks to your students based on their academic performance. Marks will be displayed to student login automatically and can be edited later.
